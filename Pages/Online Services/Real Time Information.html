What it is

One of the major benefits brought about by internet is that people are able to get updates on the news almost instantly after the event has taken place, Users are able to access the news in video, text and sound form. They can stream the news online, read it on a website or download an app that gives them regular updates on the latest news. The speed with which news is updated normally depends on how important it is.

- Flipboard (Popular news app)

Target Audience

News services are likely to be used by the majority of people with an internet access. The difference is the type of news that people choose to read about. Someone who is active in reading up on world affairs may read the political news whereas a teenager interested in music and sports may read sports and entertainment news.

Benefits and Drawbacks

Benefits

Drawbacks

News can be checked anytime and anywhere as long as there is an internet connection.

A person’s internet could crash or the website could go down for maintenance.

News is updated almost instantly on websites.

This method can be difficult for foreigners to use as websites cannot always be translated. 

News on specific topics can be found using search engines or apps such as Flipboard.

Many news websites can provide information that is biased and one-sided.

Detailed information on traffic updates and weather reports

 

 

Fitness for Purpose

In recent years, there have been complaints that teenagers today do not read the news enough. Online News Services help eradicate this problem as teenagers can use methods they are comfortable with to keep themselves updated with the news.

The reason online news services are fit for purpose is because they reach out to a large audience and this
allows important news to reach the people who do not order newspapers or watch news channels.

Non-Internet Alternatives

The alternative to using the internet to catch up on the news is to read the newspaper in its paper form. The drawback to this is that they will have to wait a whole day to read up on the news. A better option would be to watch hourly news on television. However, TV Channels may only display major news and not news that is specific to the interests of the viewer.

Possible Enhancements

To ensure that a person is able to practice his/her freedom of speech right without being criticized, all comment sections on news websites should have an anonymous feature. Furthermore, to ensure that there is no spamming in the comments, news websites should have a filter that blocks out any spam or advertising in the comments.   

What it is

The internet has also had positive effects on transport, particularly for train travelers. Online services such as train timetables can assist people in planning their route by providing them with information on the timings of train arrivals and departures. Users can browse through different options and select the train that is best for them based on timings and costs.

Target Audience

The target audience for train-timetables is are mainly people who frequently travel by train such as businessmen so that they are able to save time on the day of travel. They can also be found rather useful by people who are picking up somebody arriving by train so that they are aware of the person’s route and estimated time of arrival.


Benefits and Drawbacks

Benefits

Drawbacks

Time can be managed more efficiently by being aware of arrival and departure times.

The system can get ruined if two people book the same seat at the same time.

Your entire trip and route can be planned over the internet which reduces the chances of unexpected transport issues.

If there are problems with the system, the user may not get their information on time.

Tickets can be purchased online.

 

Travelers can browse through different trains with different timings and  select the train that best suits their needs.

 
Fitness for Purpose

Train timetables are supposed to help users save time on the day of tranzit by giving them information on departure timings and sllowing them to book their tickets online. In this regard, they are fit for purpose as the systems rarely malfunction and are generally able to provide valuable information to travellers.

Non-Internet Alternatives

The internet has changed train bookings for the better. Without the internet, people would need to buy their ticket upon reaching the station and this could create a sense of haphazardness. Alternatively, people could call up the train station and plan their schedule accordingly. However, they would be notified of any last minute changes or delays.


Possible Enhancements

Train Timetables can be improved by provding additional information about the journey such as "estimated attendance." This would be useful to many passengers who do not like travelling in trains that are too congested or too empty.

Additionally, Train Timetables should aim to improve the speed with which they update and notify users of any changes in order to avoid confusion at the station.
