Online Services - Conclusion

Scope:

The internet, if not misused is a revolutionary platform that has brought people together, education them, entertained them and has overall, made life easier. The internet has created jobs as well as helped people make their jobs easier. People rely on the internet to keep their data organised and their money safe. Some even look to it as their first choice for conducting financial transactions. The internet has enhanced the learning of students and has allowed researchers and scientists to collaborate with each other in order to move the world forward. Businesses that have made use of ICT have flourished and Governments are beginning to move payments of bills and taxes online.

Limitations:

The biggest problem with the internet is the simple fact that not everybody has access to it or is able to use it [see the digital divide section]. Aside from inability to use the internet, many people misuse the internet and use it as a medium of illegally making money, downloaded files and accessing areas not monitored by the government. Although, the internet has connected millions of people, not everybody is excited at the prospect of talking to someone you've never met online while others believe that the internet allows people to invade each other's privacy. The biggest concern for parents regarding the internet is that overusing it can lead to their children having less face-to-face contact with other people and hence, being socially isolated.
