Life in the Information Age

Whether good or bad, it is apparent that the use of ICT has impacted our professional, social and personal lives in many different ways. In this section we will look at these effects and how they've created change for better and worse. This section will also look what responsibilities and set of rules everyday users should adhere to as well as some copyright laws and regulations that users must be aware of.

In this section, I will also be mentioning how ICT has affected the lives of my Mother (Teacher), Father (Commercial director] and some of my other family members because they are first-hand witnesses of the changes brought about in their professional, social and personal lives.
