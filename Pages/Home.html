WELCOME, CITIZENS OF THE FUTURE!

If all has gone to plan and you are reading this in the year 2115, then I would assume that the majority of your generation is familiar and comfortable with robotic assistants, flying cars, jetpacks and human-controlled weather. However during this
E-book's time of creation, these ideas were mere fantasies of the human mind as 'The Information Age' was still a relatively new phenomenon and was not a concept that much of the world understood or was able to take advantage of.

This E-book was created in the year 2015 with the intetntion that it would not see the light of day until a hundred years into the future. It's purpose? To provide the future generations with a snapshot of what life was like in The Information Age.
